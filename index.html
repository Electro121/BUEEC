About Us

The BRAC University Electrical and Electronics Club is a student-driven platform that empowers students through hands-on projects, workshops, and competitions. We aim to inspire innovation and foster collaboration in the fields of electrical, electronics, and robotics engineering.

Founded in 2009, our club has grown to become one of the most active technical organizations on campus, with over 200 active members and alumni network spanning across the globe.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application through experiential learning, industry collaborations, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To become a premier platform for nurturing future leaders and innovators in electrical and electronics engineering.
