About Us

Founded in 2009, the BRAC University Electrical and Electronic Club (BUEEC) is one of the earliest clubs at BRAC University. It aims to provide its members with a safe and engaging space to interact with like-minded enthusiasts in the field of electrical and electronic engineering. The club helps members gain essential skills and self-confidence through various technical and creative activities. BUEEC supports students in participating in and improving the electrical and electronic engineering field in Bangladesh by encouraging innovation, hands-on learning, and collaborative growth.

Our Mission

The mission of BUEEC is to bridge the gap between theoretical and practical knowledge so that students can excel in developing technical competency, conducting research, and contributing to technological advancement.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant community of electrical and electronic engineering enthusiasts at BRAC University and improve the electrical and electronic engineering field in Bangladesh by encouraging innovation, hands-on learning, and collaborative growth.
